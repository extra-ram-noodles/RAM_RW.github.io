Slug cat pollo

Scugpollos predominately resemble slugcats as they're closely related, however they are much more short and stout, and lack the dexterous-ish hands of a scug, furthermore they posses a much rounder build, their fore (the section which stands erect) has a wide base that tapers in a mellow manner. Just like a marshmallow.
Unlike scugs, scugpollos are gonochoric and so are separated into males and females and not both, the difference is quite easy to tell in mature individuals, as females are much larger than males and posses a formation of fat most compareable to a fairly sized neck pillow.
Wild scugpollos are already quite the pudgy creatures, but after domestication for their eggs and meat, they became even more portly, however they were not subjected to the same artificial alterations as chickens (not in any large scale anyway), forking them off into wild scugpollos and domestic scugpollos that survived the collapse of the ancients, now called faux-wild scugpollos.
Withal some ancients remained preferring of eating wild scugpollos as they were superior in taste. And they were some of the easiest game there is, as long as you keep your distance.
